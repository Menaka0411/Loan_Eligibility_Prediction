Loan Elibility Prediction


Check Your Eligibility For Getting Various Loans From Banks




1. Fill out the Form

Enter the following information into the corresponding input fields:


Gender: Enter '1' for Male or '0' for Female.
Married: Enter '1' for Yes or '0' for No.
Education: Enter '0' for Not Educated or '1' for UG or '2' for PG or
'3' for Below 10th or '4' for SSLC or '5' for HSC
Self Employed: Enter '1' for Yes or '0' for No.
Applicant Income: Enter the applicant's income.
Coapplicant Income: Enter the coapplicant's income.
Loan Amount: Enter the loan amount required.
Loan Amount Term: Enter the loan amount term (in months).
Credit History: Enter '1' for Good credit history or '0' for Bad credit history.
Property Area: Enter '0' for Rural, '1' for Semiurban, or '2' for Urban.

2. Verify and Predict

Check all entered details for accuracy. Once verified, click the "Predict" button to submit your application.



3. Displaying Results

After clicking the "Predict" button, the results of your loan eligibility will be displayed based on the provided information.
